COST MODEL FIX
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

BEFORE (WRONG):
  positions = y_pred * 2 - 1
  pnl = positions * returns
  net_return = pnl.sum()

AFTER (CORRECT):
  positions = y_pred * 2 - 1
  position_changes = abs(diff(positions))
  costs = position_changes * spread_bps / 10000
  pnl = positions * returns - costs
  net_return = pnl.sum()

SPREAD ASSUMPTION:
  XAUUSD spread: 0.3-0.5 USD on ~2000 price
  = 0.015-0.025% = 1.5-2.5 bps one-way
  = 3-5 bps round-trip
  Used: 5 bps (conservative)
